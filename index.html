Meet the new Mi Mix 2

Full screen display

Experience true immersive full display design with Mi MIX 2. The chin is 12% smaller than its predecessor Mi MIX and the display uses an 18:9 aspect ratio - a result of our relentless pursuit of technology innovation.

Four-sided curved ceramic body

Mi MIX 2 is dressed to impress with a four-sided curved ceramic back plate and 18-karat gold-plated decorative camera rim, yet is highly durable with an aerospace-grade aluminum alloy frame.

Flagship-level camera

Mi MIX 2 uses an IMX386 rear camera sensor, large 1.25μm pixels, and 4-axis optical image stabilization to help take gorgeous photos in all situations.
